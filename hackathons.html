Hackathons and Achievements

About

Information about all the hackathons and other competitions that I've been a part of over the course of my undergraduate education.

University of Twente, Netherlands (Exchange Program)

I will be spending the first half of 2021 at Enschede, Netherlands. Its because I've bagged an opportunity to take up graduate-level courses and project-work at the University of Twente,Netherlands as a part of Amrita's exchange program. The amazing courses which I will be taking there include Advanced Computer Vision, Information retrieval, Time Series Analysis and many others. Some of these courses come with a project component and that is going to make this entire journey way more exciting.
Insider news: A solo europe trip is very much possible. Stick around for updates :)

Quantum Computing program by IBM & The Coding School.

The Coding School in collaboration with IBM started an initiative to teach selected students both theoretical as well as programming concepts in Quantum Computing via a six month long program. Quantum Computing is an exciting field and it works on principles that are not only different from classical computing but in many ways also better than it. I am lucky to be one among those selected for this program and look forward to experimenting with the knowledge I'll gain from this.

IEEE Yesist12 Makerfair 2020

Makerfair 2020 was an international competition with participants from more than 30 countries. We received an honorary mention for our project, “Jatayu: An AI-based Criminal Tagging System”, thus placing us in the top 5 out of 34 other finalists.

National Innovation Contest 2020 (Ongoing)

NIC 2020 is a national level competition conducted by the Govt. of India. It is open to colleges and universities across the country. We successfully passed through two rounds of evaluation, the ideation round and the prototype round. We are currently in the national level mentoring round.

Smart India Hackathon 2020

Smart India Hackathon 2020 was a national level hackathon, conducted by the Govt. of India.It is open to students across all colleges and universities. We progressed through three rounds of evaluation, the Ideation round and the Prototype round at the University level and the finale at the national level. We finished in the third place out of five finalists in national finals.

Infineon hack 2019

Infineon hackathon was a Machine Learning hackathon that was open to everyone across the nation from professionals to university/college students. We finished in the 4th position with our machine learning model for Document classification.

SLAC Honeywell hackathon 2019

SLAC was a University-level hackathon with participants from all the branches of our University. We built a false fire alarm detection system using blockchain and deep learning and finished among the top 10 finalists with that project.

Impactathon 2019

Impactathon 2019 was an industry-level hackathon with most of the participants from the tech industry. It was conducted by Appdynamics, A Cisco Company. The theme was to build low-cost product that can be useful for NGO's fighting climate change. We built a low cost drone-based environmental data collection system that can replace the reliance of these organizations on major environmental data providers. We finished as the finalists in this hackathon.

Smart India Hackathon 2019

Smart India Hackathon 2019 was a national level hackathon, conducted by the Govt. of India. It is open to students across all colleges and universities. We progressed through two rounds of evaluation, the Ideation round and the finale at the national level. We finished as the finalists in national finals.

Amrita Vidyanidhi merit scholarship

The Amrita Vidyanidhi merit scholarship is given to students who achieved high ranks in the Amrita Entrance Examination and it gets renewed every year if the student manages to maintain an excellent academic standing. I've been a recipient of this scholarship throughout my undergraduate education.
